Characters

  • King Lear
  • The aging, prideful King of England
  • He is a controlling man and does not like being challenged
  • King Lear disowns his daughter because she refuses to say she loves him, and in doing so sets the tragedies course
  • Goneril
  • King Lear's older daughter, she is a very aggressive and nasty woman
  • She seeks her father's power, as well as control over her husband, eventually when her plan fails, she kills herself.
  • Cordelia
  • Cordelia is Lear's youngest daughter
  • She is disowned by her father
  • She marries the King of France
  • Dies after her sisters send her to prison
  • Gloucester
  • Gloucester is a very loyal Earl
  • Had an affair and fathered a bastard son named Edmund
  • Very similar to Lear (in the way they both betray their children)
  • Drives away his true son, Edgar
  • King Lear's daughters strip him of his title
  • Regan
  • The middle daughter of King Lear
  • She is a very ruthless and similar to her sister Goneril
  • Edgar
  • Oldest son of Gloucester
  • Gloucester's actual son
  • He is very gullible
  • Defeats his brother, Edmund
  • Edmund
  • Illegitimate son of Gloucester
  • He is very jealous of Edgar and is a bitter person
  • He wants to take away everything from Edgar and Gloucester
